Privacy Policy

Last updated: [DATE TO BE UPDATED]

This Privacy Policy explains how Virtual Try-On ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our browser extension and website.

Information We Collect

Extension Data

  • Photos you upload: Stored locally in your browser's extension storage. We do not receive or transmit your photos to our servers unless you explicitly connect a third-party model provider (e.g., OpenRouter).
  • API keys: If you opt in, your API key is stored locally in extension storage and is not transmitted to our servers. It is sent only to the configured model provider to fulfill your requests.
  • Extension settings: Your preferences and settings are stored locally on your device.

Website Analytics

  • Usage data: We may collect anonymized usage analytics on the website (not in the extension) to improve content and performance.
  • Technical information: Browser type, operating system, and general location data may be collected for analytics purposes.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide core extension features (upload, manage photos, generate try-on results)
  • Communicate with the AI model provider you configure, when you initiate generation
  • Improve the website's content and performance
  • Provide customer support when requested
  • Ensure the security and proper functioning of our services

Data Sharing and Disclosure

Third-Party Services

  • AI model providers: We share data only with the AI model provider you configure, and only to fulfill your requests.
  • Analytics services: We may use third-party analytics services that collect anonymized data.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, regulation, or legal process, or to protect our rights and the rights of others.

We do not sell personal data to third parties.

Data Retention

  • Extension data: Photos and API keys remain on your device until you delete them via the extension settings.
  • Website analytics: Retained per our analytics vendor's default retention policies (typically 26 months).
  • Support communications: Retained for as long as necessary to provide support and comply with legal obligations.

Your Privacy Rights and Choices

Extension Data Control

  • You can delete stored photos and API keys at any time in the extension's Settings
  • You can uninstall the extension to remove all local data
  • You can choose which AI providers to connect with

Website Analytics

  • You can opt out of analytics with browser settings or ad blockers
  • You can enable "Do Not Track" in your browser

Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

  • Local storage encryption for sensitive data
  • Secure transmission protocols (HTTPS)
  • Regular security assessments
  • Limited access to personal information

Important: No method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are under 13, please do not use our service or provide any information to us.

If we learn we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will delete that information as quickly as possible.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure that such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws and implement appropriate safeguards.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Notify users through the extension or website
  • For significant changes, provide additional notice as required by law

Your continued use of our service after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
